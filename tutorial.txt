How to install the Vortex Anti-Bot on your landing page

1. Rename your index.html for index2.html

2. Unzip the folder and upload the contents to your landing root.

3. You should now have: index.html index2.html 404.html secure.png

4. Edit your index2.html (Landing Page) and paste this
  <script src="web.js"></script>

5. You have successfully installed the anti bot you can now host your website
